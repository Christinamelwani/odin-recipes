Garlic fried Rice

Go back to all recipes

Description:

Couldn't miss adding my favourite type of fried rice,

Ingredients

  • 8 clove garlic, sliced
  • 1-2 serving rice
  • 1-2 pc egg
  • ¼ pc onion, diced into ½-cm squares
  • 1-2 pc green onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp stock powder / dashi powder
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • ½ tbsp flour
  • ½ pc carrot, diced into 3-mm squares
  • ⅙ pc red bell pepper, diced

Steps:

  1. Slice the garlic. Coat with a little bit of flour and fry until golden. Set aside.
  2. Heat the pan again. Add the eggs in and stir, then add the onion, carrots, bell peppers, and green onions. Mix well.
  3. Add the rice in and stir. Then, add the soy sauce, dashi powder, sugar, pepper, and butter in. Mix well.
  4. For the green onion garnish, rinse with cold water.
  5. Serve the Garlic Fried Rice with fried garlic and green onions.
